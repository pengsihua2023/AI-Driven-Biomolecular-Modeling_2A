# Defining AI Advantage for This Proposal

AI advantage in this Phase I project should mean more than using a modern model.

## Operational definition
AI advantage is demonstrated if the structure-informed AI workflow shows measurable improvement over simpler baselines in one or more of the following:
- predictive performance on predefined benchmark tasks
- calibration and uncertainty estimation
- robustness across held-out mutation patterns or clades
- interpretability of structure-function signals
- efficiency in identifying high-priority mutation patterns for follow-up analysis

## Non-acceptable definition
Do not define AI advantage as:
- “using a transformer”
- “training on large data”
- “better visualization”
- generic claims of automation without quantitative evidence
