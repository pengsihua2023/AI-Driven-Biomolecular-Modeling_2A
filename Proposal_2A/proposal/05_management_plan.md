# Management Plan

## Management model
Use a lean Phase I management structure:
- PI: overall scientific leadership, sponsor communication, milestone accountability
- Co-PI / Lab Lead: DOE-aligned biomolecular science coordination
- Technical Lead: data/model workflow integration
- Project Manager or designated coordinator: schedule, deliverables, meeting documentation

## Recommended cadence
- weekly technical working meeting
- biweekly PI/co-PI milestone review
- monthly cross-institution review with action log
- month 3, 6, and 9 formal milestone checkpoint

## Required management artifacts
- milestone tracker
- decision log
- risk register
- dataset provenance record
- model versioning record
- deliverable checklist
