# DOE Phase I Rules for This Proposal

## Confirmed from FOA
- RFA: `DE-FOA-0003612`
- Phase I duration: **9 months**
- Phase I expected award range: **$500,000–$750,000**
- Phase I requires **multi-institutional teams**
- Team must span at least **two eligible partner categories**
- Phase I goal is to demonstrate a **clear, tangible research workflow** and evaluate the potential for **AI advantage**

## Topic-specific fit
Topic 2 is biotechnology-focused. Focus Area 2A asks applicants to determine principles connecting **protein structure** with **function**.

## Writing implications
The narrative should emphasize:
- biomolecular mechanism
- structure-informed modeling
- functional relevance
- benchmarked scientific workflow
- quantitative evidence

The narrative should not rely only on:
- disease burden language
- general public health urgency
- black-box prediction claims without mechanistic framing
