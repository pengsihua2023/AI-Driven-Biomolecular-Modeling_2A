# Teaming Plan

## Phase I teaming requirement
The FOA requires a multi-institutional team. The final proposal must include partners from at least two eligible categories.

## Recommended minimum team shape
- **Lead academic institution or non-profit**: scientific integration, model development, benchmark design, proposal leadership
- **DOE lab or Scientific User Facility partner**: biomolecular science expertise, data/infrastructure/method support, DOE mission alignment
- **Optional industry partner**: model engineering, compute/data tooling, translational perspective, or experimental relevance

## Teaming principles
- keep the team small enough for a 9-month project
- every partner must have a necessary intellectual role
- do not add “logo partners” without substantive contribution
- map each milestone to a responsible institution

## Letter needs
- letters of commitment from funded partners
- letters of collaboration or access from unfunded contributors if relevant
- avoid generic endorsement letters
