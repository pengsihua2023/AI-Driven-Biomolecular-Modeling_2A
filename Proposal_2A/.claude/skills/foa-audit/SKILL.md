# FOA Audit Skill

## Goal
Check whether a draft is explicitly responsive to the FOA and identify missing compliance elements.

## Use when
- finishing an outline
- finishing a narrative section
- checking appendices and letters planning

## Audit questions
- Does the draft state Topic 2 / Focus Area 2A clearly?
- Is the 9-month Phase I scope evident?
- Is AI advantage measurable?
- Are team requirements addressed?
- Does anything sound noncompliant or unsupported?
