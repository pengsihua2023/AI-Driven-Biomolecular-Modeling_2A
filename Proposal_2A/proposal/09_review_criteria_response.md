# Reviewer Criteria Response Notes

## Likely review dimensions
- responsiveness to FOA and 2A
- scientific merit and originality
- technical approach and feasibility
- evidence of AI advantage potential
- team strength and appropriateness
- management and deliverability

## Questions the draft must answer cleanly
- Why is this a BER biomolecular science problem?
- What exactly will be delivered in 9 months?
- How is AI advantage measured?
- Why is this team necessary and credible?
- Why is DOE the right sponsor?

## Common weaknesses to avoid
- scope too broad for Phase I
- generic AI rhetoric
- insufficient benchmark specificity
- poor explanation of structure-function connection
- missing or weak baseline comparisons
- unclear team role separation
