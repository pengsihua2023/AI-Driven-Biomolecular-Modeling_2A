# Risk Register

## Scientific risks
- Sparse or inconsistent labels for escape- or adaptation-relevant outcomes
- Structural uncertainty for some variants or regions
- Weak coupling between benchmark labels and real biological mechanism

## Technical risks
- Overfitting due to limited high-value benchmark data
- Representation complexity not translating to measurable gains
- Poor calibration despite apparent discrimination performance

## Management risks
- partner overextension in a short Phase I period
- data access or harmonization delays
- unclear deliverable ownership

## Mitigation themes
- define minimal viable tasks early
- include strong baselines
- use ablations to test whether structure adds value
- treat uncertainty explicitly
- prioritize one benchmark-quality workflow over excessive breadth
